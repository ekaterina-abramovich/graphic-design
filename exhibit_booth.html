Description

The project focused on designing an exhibit booth for Insilico Medicine, a company specializing in advanced computational methods to accelerate drug discovery and development. The primary objective was to create a visually appealing and impactful design that aligned with Insilico's corporate branding.

The design theme was kept simple yet striking, incorporating Insilico's logo, fonts, and color palette, with a preference for darker tones. Decorative elements such as DNA polymers, chemical compounds, and molecules were integrated to emphasize the company's connection to medicine and bioengineering. Additionally, the booth prominently featured the company slogan, "Artificial Intelligence for Every Step of Discovery," displayed under the logo.
