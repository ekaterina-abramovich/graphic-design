Introduction
about project & brand

Rouge Bunny Rouge is a luxury cosmetics brand known for its whimsical and enchanting branding inspired by fairy tales and fantasy themes. The company offers a range of high-quality makeup and skincare products, including foundations, lipsticks, eyeshadows, and skincare items. The brand aims to provide a unique and immersive experience for its customers, combining beauty with storytelling.

For this specific photoshoot project, my role as a designer involved three stages: capturing high-quality images of each individual product, capturing macro shots of each product's content and textures, and using photo retouching techniques to creatively combine both shot types. Those designs was later used in social media posts and other promotional materials. The project featured six different products.
