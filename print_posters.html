poster #1

The first poster aims to invite students to socialize, play games, and enjoy free food. Highlighting the availability of free food was crucial, as events offering this tend to attract more attendees. To emphasize this, the design is prominently featuring a slice of pizza, underscoring the fun and inviting atmosphere of the event.

Another promotional poster was created for the Journal Open Mic event to attract a student audience. The poster highlighted that invited writers would read their work aloud.

Hosted by Lang's Eleven and a Half Literary Journal, the design incorporated elements from the Journal‚Äôs asset library, including a black-and-white checker pattern and yellow color. Additionally, an illustration of a microphone was featured to emphasize the event's focus on live readings.

Poster #3

The final print poster was created for a workshop event led by Professor Chang from the Journalism and Design program. The workshop was supposed to guide beginning journalists on how to create a portfolio website.

The design prominently featured a photo of Professor Chang, making it easy for his past or current students to recognize him and be encouraged to attend the event. To add a creative touch, I incorporated a header element at the top of the poster, giving it the appearance of a website landing page.
